After School Program

Welcome to our after school program! We offer a variety of activities for children of all ages.

At Class Measures Limited, we are passionate about empowering young minds through creative, fun, and impactful learning experiences. Since our founding in 2019, we’ve worked closely with schools and parents to provide high-quality afterschool programs and learning materials that spark curiosity, build confidence, and nurture essential 21st-century skills.

Our programs are designed to be engaging, educational, and enjoyable, ensuring that children not only learn but also have fun while doing so. We believe that every child has the potential to excel, and we are committed to helping them reach their full potential.

Join us in our mission to inspire the next generation of thinkers, creators, and leaders!
